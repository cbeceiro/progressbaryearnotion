How to use in Notion

  1. 1
    Choose a widget
    Open any card below and copy its URL.
  2. 2
    Type /embed
    In your Notion page, create an Embed block.
  3. 3
    Paste the URL
    Paste the widget link and press Enter.
  4. 4
    Adjust size
    Resize the block until it looks the way you want.
